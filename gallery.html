My Ditigil Imaging project
MMP 100 | Digital Imagining Project
Title: Selfie with me and the stars
Semester: Fall/2019
Software: Photoshop
Description: For the raster project, I wanted to pick images that best described me in as little words as possible, after all a picture is worth a thousand words. The background of the si-fy plant represents the future and where we are heading, and the second background represents the scope we have towards the vast seemingly unlimited sky. and the blend between the backgrounds shows how we are creating the future that we are seeking. The person on the hoverboard represents a hobby of mine (electric longboarding) together the picture shows the past on the bottom and the future on top and while I travel into the future I'll pick up some hobbies alone the way.


MMP 100 | Digital Imagining Project
Title: vector based slife portrait
Semester: Fall/2019
Software: Illustrator
Description: For this project, I tried recreating the same self-portrait. I started by imagining a world were mirrors didn't exist, Then I closed my eyes and imagined how I looked like and tried my best to recreate that image. For this picture, I picked my favorite color since it reminds me of water. When I see blue and can hear the sounds of water flowing it helps me forget all my troubles as if the water is washing my emotions away. Throughout this projects, drawing with vectors was extremely hard. in the real-life, there are a lot of natural shapes that cannot be re-done with a geometric shape. I found that hair was the hardest thing to do, I was only able to over-come this by using the curvature pen tool. and meticulously adding small strands of hair together.



Audio project
MMP 100 | Audio Project
Title: Dreaming of a dream
Semester: Fall/2019
Software: Audacity
Description: it is human nature to rely on all our senses (if we have them), but what happens when we lose one of our senses? Do our other senses become stronger, does our body find a way to function without them? When I started the project I knew I wanted to present the idea of a dream, after all when we are dreaming, anything is possible. Without our sight and only our hearing left, can we still remember what’s it like walking around?
